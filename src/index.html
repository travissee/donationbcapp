Peace of Giving was founded in Singapore by John and Jane who were active members of the local charity community. Together with their interest in blockchain technology, they aspired to create a platform for donators to donate without borders and allow the charities to receive the donation instantly.

Please note that 1% of the donation will be used to maintain the operations of this service.
